## EVALUATION CRITERIA FOR EACH SCENARIO

### 1. Scientific Accuracy (0-25 points)
- Accurate clinical data
- Appropriate bibliographic references
- Correct medical terminology

### 2. Communication Clarity (0-25 points)
- Structured and logical message
- Adaptation to professional's level
- Conciseness and relevance

### 3. Objection Handling (0-25 points)
- Active listening
- Honest and transparent answers
- Ability to recover positively

### 4. Regulatory Compliance (0-25 points)
- Adherence to approved claims
- Appropriate mention of adverse effects
- No excessive promises

**TOTAL SCORE: /100**

## KEY PHRASES TO USE (RECOMMENDATIONS)

### Professional Opening
- "Thank you for seeing me, Dr. [Name]. I really appreciate your time."
- "I know you're very busy, I'll be brief."

### Presenting Benefits
- "Our pivotal study demonstrated..."
- "The data published in [journal] shows that..."
- "Compared to standard treatment, we observe..."

### Handling Objections
- "That's an excellent question. Let me give you the precise data..."
- "I understand your concern. Here's what our studies reveal..."
- "You're right to raise that point..."

### Professional Closure
- "May I leave you this scientific documentation?"
- "Would you be open to me following up in [timeframe]?"
- "Thank you for this discussion. How would you prefer I keep you informed?"
